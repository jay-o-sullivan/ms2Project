About


Wonderfully spacious, perfectly placed and incredibly enticing, Abbey Rock Resort is nestled in the beautiful grounds of the 900 Acre forestry and lakeside. At Abbey Rock Resort we invite our guests to enjoy the many activities we have to offer and it is the perfect retreat to get back to nature, relax and take time to enjoy the company of family and friends in these beautiful surroundings.
We only take limited bookings at a time so we can keep all our guests and visitors safe.

Activities

What we offer


Golf

why not play in our fantastic 18 hole golf course or have some fun with the kids in some crazy golf.

Hiking/Walking

Maybe a nice walk around the forest or some hiking through the mountains and we also have a beautiful cycle track where you might see some of our local wildlife.

Fishing

Maybe catch a few fish for the barbeque or just relax and unwind. 2 fish per person

Whale Watching

Why not try our boat trip and see some whales up close in the wild and its also a fantastic opportunity to get some photos. we offer 3 boat trips a day.

Pet Farm

Come and see some of our cute and furry friends and why not purchase some of our pet food so you can feed them.

Nature

Enjoy nature at its finest whether its relaxing with some music or just reading a book
