About


Abbey Rock Resort was founded in 2020, during the covid19 lockdown. It is a place to getaway while in lockdown and is abiding by all procedures. This means people can have a break away from home and enjoy all we have to offer, it has enough space for everyone to have a good time and not worry about the pandemic. Everything is sanitised including facilities and all of our activities are based outside in open so 2 meters apart is used.
